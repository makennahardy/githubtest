Muffins

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups frozen rasberries
  • 2 tablespoons flaxseed meal
  • 1 1/4 cups oatmilk
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 3/4 cups all purpose flour (345g)
  • 2 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • pinch of salt (about .5 teaspoons)
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1/4 cups brown sugar
  • 3 tablespoons oil
  • 3 tablespoon vegan butter, melted
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract

    Instructions

  1. Wash blueberries and dry with towel. Set aside.
  2. Preheat oven to 425ºF/218ºC. Grease 12 cup standard muffin tin.
  3. In small bowl, whisk flaxseed meal and 5 tablespoon warm water. Let rest for 15 minutes.
  4. In another bowl, mix lemon juice and oatmilk. Let Rest for 5-10 minutes.
  5. Mix ingredients. Make sure to fold in blueberries last.
  6. Fill each muffin cup with batter.
  7. Bake at 425ºF/218ºC for six minutes. Without opening the door, reduce the heat to 350ºF/175ºC and bake for 16- 18 more minutes.
  8. Transfer to cooling rack and let cool for 20 minutes upon removing from oven.
  9. Enjoy!
