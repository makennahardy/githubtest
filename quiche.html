Muffins

Ingredients

  • 2 9 inch pie crusts
  • 1 finely chopped green pepper
  • 1 finely chopped onion
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vegetable oil

    Instructions

  1. In a blender combine eggs, milk, flour, salt and oil. Process until smooth. Cover and refrigerate 1 hour.
  2. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and brush with oil. Cook 2 to 5 minutes, turning once, until golden. Repeat with remaining batter.
  3. Pour 1/4 cup of crepe batter into pan, tilting to completely coat the surface of the pan. For mini crepes, 2 tablespoons and only coat the bottom of the pan.
  4. Cook 2 to 5 minutes, turning once, until golden.
  5. Repeat with remaining batter. Alternatively, batter can be stored for up to a week in refrigerator.
  6. Garnish. Some recommended toppings are hazelnut chocolate spread, strawberries, or whipped cream
